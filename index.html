Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify an earwig infestation?

Look for signs like damaged plants, small holes in leaves, and earwigs in damp, dark areas around your home.

Are earwigs dangerous?

Earwigs are generally harmless to humans but can damage plants and be a nuisance when they enter homes.

How long does treatment take?

Most treatments show results within 1-2 weeks, with proper prevention methods ensuring long-term control.
