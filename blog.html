Complete Guide: How to Get Rid of Earwigs Naturally

Discover effective, natural solutions for eliminating earwigs from your home and garden. These nocturnal insects thrive in dark, damp environments but can be controlled through simple preventive measures. Learn to protect your space using diatomaceous earth, oil traps, and boric acid solutions, while implementing long-term strategies like moisture reduction and proper garden maintenance. The guide covers essential prevention methods, including sealing entry points, maintaining drainage, and using natural repellents like marigolds. Whether dealing with a minor presence or severe infestation, this comprehensive approach helps you tackle earwig problems safely and effectively.
